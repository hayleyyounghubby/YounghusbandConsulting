who we are

We are a team of professionals with over twenty-five years of experience in the field of fire protection engineering and fire & life safety code consulting.

All of our projects are unique and call for distinct research on your particular buildings and innovative solutions to guarantee you meet all relevant code requirements. Using our detailed analysis, our team could save you time and money as you proceed with your project.

Building codes become longer and more complex with each coming year. We are experts trained to understand and interpret these changing model building codes to ensure safety for the occupants of your building.

What we do

We leverage our extensive knowledge of the building code, fire protection engineering science, and strong relationships with approving agencies to help our Clients achieve their pro-forma and design objectives in a timely and cost effective manner.

Involving us early and throughout the design process allows us to identify compliance issues and develop creative solutions prior to plan check thereby minimizing costly delays.
